About

I’m Sam, a 31-year-old man from Argentina, and that’s my catified rubber duck.


I’m a self-taught developer. Halfway through my math degree I completed Harvard’s CS50 . After reading The C Programming Language by K&R, I knew I had found my vocation in computer science.


Having decided to pursue a career in web development, Full Stack Open by the University of Helsinki was the next step. I found both the flexibility and potential of the web fascinating, and that’s how I got here.


Before all that, I worked as Software QA and NOC operator.
